About

I currently work as a Character Animator at Little Chicken Game Company, an Amsterdam based studio that has been designing award winning games since 2001. I've got my start in games as a Producer where I gained a lot of valuable experience about creative team leadership. But a lot of looong nights were spent on my passion: animation. So when the opportunity to work as a 3d animator presented itself in 2014, I never looked back.

And now I've started the next phase of my career, by learning to code. I strive to create unique experiences that have an impact on people, or better yet, to spark the imagination of others through storytelling. And I feel that the modern superpower of programming will enable me to do so even more.

Wish me luck!
